My Experience

Since 2021 I've composed instrumental game tracks on-and-off for small developers on Roblox. Unfortunately, most of the projects I worked for later died during development, so my work never saw publicity :(.

I specialize in orchestral and instrumental tracks for a variety of musical roles in your game. I can compose for ambience, for action, and across a broad spectrum of emotions. I can compose music across a variety of Western historical styles, from 18th century baroque all the way to modern day orchestral work, and anything in between.

Besides composing, I've also arranged music for different ensembles for a long time. The bulk of my arrangements can be found at my Musescore.
